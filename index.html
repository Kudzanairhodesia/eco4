ECO4: TOWARDS NET

ECO4 is the fourth phase of the Energy Company Obligation scheme, a government-funded program that aims to improve the energy efficiency of homes in the UK. The schemes provides funding for a range of energy-saving measures, such as insulations, boiler upgrades, and renewable energy installations.Our mission is to create a clean future for all.

Mission Statement

The UK government has introduced the ECO4
scheme so homeowners, private tenants and
landlords can apply for a government grant. ECO4 is
committed to creating a sustainable future by
reducing greenhouse gas emission and being eco-
friendly.Anybody living in a property that was not
eligible for the ECO4 scheme missed out on energy
savings. ECO4 intends to reduce carbon emissions
and empower communities to take charge of their
own environment. The scheme has access to
funding from the UK government and covers for all
the cost. Another objective is to reduce fuel
poverty by using renewable and clean energy like
solar energy.
